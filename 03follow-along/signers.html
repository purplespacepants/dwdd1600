Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
He was the principal author of the Declaration and later became the third president of the United States.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, Adams went on to become the second president of the U.S.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned polymath, Franklin was an influential figure in securing support for the colonies and a key diplomat.
Samuel Adams
A leading figure in organizing resistance against British rule and a cousin of John Adams.
John Hancock
Known for his bold signature, Hancock was the president of the Continental Congress at the time and became the first governor of Massachusetts.
Richard Henry Lee
He proposed the resolution for independence in June 1776, leading to the Declaration.
